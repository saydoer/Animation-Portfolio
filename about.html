About

Charlotte Denton is a 2D animator and game designer based in Atlanta, GA. Charlotte attended the Georgia Institute of Technology where she received a Master of Sciences in Digital Media. She has spent her time working on numerous indie games and animations with clients, friends, and herself.

Although most of my work is in character art and animation, I have an insatiable drive for good interaction design and unconventional player experiences. Whether it’s learning a new asset pipe-line or diving into code, I am excited to find ways to move out of my comfort zone to be a more complete developer.

I am deeply activated by games. I grew up hooked on series like Pokémon and standout titles like Lost Magic, while later on I discovered my true love in indie games such as Limbo, Hollow Knight, and Signalis. After graduating from Georgia Tech with a Master of Sciences degree in Digital Media, I returned to my passion for gaming by entering game jams and developing various passion projects.

My hobbies are currently: fencing, reading about marine animals, designing characters, rediscovering music composition, and spending time with my partner.

I’ve lived in a handful of countries across 4 continents, visited many more, and now live in East Atlanta.
